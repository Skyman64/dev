... The elastic modulus is a measure of stress (force over a given area) over strain (extension over original length) and therefore has pressure units (GPa in the table). A smaller value indicates that the material is more flexible/compliant ...

The yield strength of a material is the stress at which a material begins to deform plastically (i.e. it will not return to its original configuration). ... If a material deforms plastically, it is no longer considered compliant

When selecting a material, look for a proportionally low elastic modulus and high yield strength.
