Probability Definitions

Shannon Entropy

$$ H(p) = - \sum_{x} p(x) \lg p(x) $$

Cross Entropy

$$ H(p,q) = - \sum_{x} p(x) \lg q(x) $$

Kullback-Leilbler Divergence

$$ \begin{array}{ll} D_{KL} (p ||q) & = - \sum_{k} p(x) \lg \frac{q(x)}{p(x)} \\ & = - \left( \sum_{x} p(x) \lg q(x) - p(x) \lg p(x) \right) \end{array} $$
